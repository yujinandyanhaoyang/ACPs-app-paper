摘　要

图书个性化推荐对于缓解数字阅读场景中的信息过载、提升读者检索效率和优化阅读服务质量具有重要意义。该任务面临用户行为稀疏且动态漂移、图书语义信息复杂以及推荐过程需要兼顾准确性与可解释性的多重挑战。为应对上述问题，本文围绕读者画像建模、书目内容理解、策略仲裁优化和系统实现开展研究。本文的主要工作包括：

1. 针对用户行为稀疏和兴趣随时间漂移的问题，设计并实现了一种基于时间衰减与语义归纳的读者画像建模方法。该方法对近 90 天行为事件进行衰减加权，并在低样本场景下引入大语言模型完成题材语义归纳，生成 256 维画像向量、置信度和冷启动标记，提高了稀疏场景下的画像鲁棒性。
2. 针对图书内容语义复杂和多目标排序难以统一协调的问题，设计并实现了一种基于内容提案与上下文赌博机仲裁的推荐方法。该方法利用文本嵌入、投影表示和 Jensen-Shannon 散度生成内容权重建议，再通过上下文赌博机动态输出排序参数，提升了推荐策略对不同用户状态和偏好偏移场景的适应能力。
3. 实现了多路召回与 MMR 多样性重排机制。系统同时使用 FAISS 语义召回和 ALS 协同过滤召回，从内容相似度和协同信号两个维度扩大候选集覆盖范围，并在排序阶段引入 MMR 重排平衡相关性和多样性。
4. 构建了一个基于 ACPs 协议的图书个性化推荐系统 Reading Concierge。系统以 Reading Concierge 为 Leader，协同读者画像、书目内容、推荐决策、推荐引擎和反馈五类 Partner，完成注册发现、任务编排、候选召回、排序解释和反馈闭环等功能，为 ACPs 协议在推荐场景中的落地提供了可复现的系统方案。实验结果表明，本文系统在准确性指标上优于传统推荐基线，并在多样性方面表现出较好的潜力。

关键词　个性化推荐；ACPs协议；多智能体协作；图书推荐；上下文赌博机

ABSTRACT

Personalized book recommendation is important for alleviating information overload in digital reading services and improving user access to relevant reading materials. The task is challenging because user behavior signals are sparse and drifting, book semantics are complex, and the recommendation process must balance accuracy with explainability. To address these issues, this thesis studies reader profiling, content understanding, strategy arbitration, and system implementation for book recommendation. The main contributions are as follows:

1. To address sparse user behavior and temporal preference drift, a reader profiling method based on time decay and semantic induction is designed and implemented. The method reweights recent behavior events within a 90-day window and introduces a large language model (LLM) based genre induction step in low-sample scenarios, producing a 256-dimensional profile vector, a confidence score, and a cold-start flag, which improves profile robustness under sparse data conditions.
2. To address complex book semantics and the difficulty of coordinating multiple ranking objectives, a recommendation method based on content proposals and contextual bandit arbitration is designed and implemented. The method combines text embeddings, projected content representations, and Jensen-Shannon divergence to generate content-side weighting suggestions, and then uses a contextual bandit to adapt execution parameters for different user states and preference-shift scenarios.
3. A multi-source recall and MMR diversity reranking mechanism is implemented. The system employs both FAISS semantic recall and ALS collaborative filtering recall to expand candidate coverage from content similarity and collaborative signals, and introduces MMR reranking to balance relevance and diversity.
4. A personalized book recommendation system named Reading Concierge is constructed based on the ACPs protocol. With Reading Concierge as the Leader and five specialized Partners for reader profiling, book content analysis, recommendation decision, recommendation execution, and feedback processing, the system implements service registration and discovery, task orchestration, candidate recall, ranking explanation, and feedback-driven updates, providing a reproducible ACPs-based solution for recommendation scenarios. Experimental results demonstrate that the proposed system achieves superior accuracy compared to traditional recommendation baselines and shows promising diversity performance.

KEY WORDS  personalized recommendation; ACPs protocol; multi-agent collaboration; book recommendation; contextual bandit
